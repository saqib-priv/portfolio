Attendance Reminder

Automated detection + email verification for incomplete attendance records.

Overview

Processes monthly attendance exports, pivots the raw layout into a table, detects rows with missing Time-In or Time-Out and sends templated emails for verification. Designed for HR ops — simple to run and auditable.

How it works

  1. Script reads the latest attendance Excel export.
  2. Transforms the data into clean rows (GT ID, Name, Department, Time In, Time Out).
  3. Flags incomplete records and creates a summary Excel.
  4. Sends templated emails to employees & CC managers (after manager approval).
